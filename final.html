National Abortion Data

Fluctuation based on state, age, and year
By Hannah Karlin

Summary of the Dataset:

Today, the phrase “You have a choice,” cannot be said with confidence. This dataset looks at the number of abortions performed within each U.S. State-further categorized by age range and year. The years analyzed include a decade's difference of 2010 and 2020. Now more than ever, abortion attains prominence as legal conflicts persist. On June 24, 2022, Roe v. Wade was overturned, taking away the female right to abortion. Additionally, the availability of birth control is being stripped by certain states.


My findings deem newsworthy as they can depict the results of similar legal challenges within the past decade. It's critical to analyze such data as it reveals progression and regression regarding women's reproductive rights-a battle being fought since the 1900’s.
